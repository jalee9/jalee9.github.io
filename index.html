About

A place to share both the work itself and the values behind it.

I teach web development and information technology at the community college level, where much of my work centers on helping students build confidence with technical skills that can genuinely change their lives.

I am especially interested in human-centered online teaching, practical uses of AI, digital pedagogy, and the quiet leadership that often happens through encouragement, clarity, and consistent support. This space brings those threads together in one place.
